Our Government Structure

The Ashtrokan Wandenreich Empire is governed by a diarchy, a system with two kings to lead the nation. The titles of these kings are the Astrukan, and Grand Falcon. Currently these positions are filled by Goomson Adan, and Coolworld Draekar

Key Government Institutions

  • Astrukan and Grand Falcon: At the head of the nation is the Astrukan and Grand Falcon, these two leaders are responsible for leading the nation through thick and thin
  • Senate: The Senate is a democratically elected legislative body responsible for creating laws for the nation
  • Ministries: Various ministries oversee specific sectors such as Development, Finances, Defense, and more. They work to develop policies and initiatives that contribute to the well-being and progress of our citizens.
  • Imperial Counciller: The Imperials Councilers are the sidekicks of the Ministers of the nation. when a minister wants something done it is up to the imperial councilers to get it done.

Our Values and Principles

The government of the Ashtrokan Wandenreich Empire is guided by the following values and principles:

  • Transparency and Accountability
  • Equality and Justice for All
  • Protection of Human Rights
  • Sustainable Development
  • Domination in International politics
